Tyler Raymond Harvey was born in the Dayton Ohio Hospital at 8:00 pm in May 31, 1990.

Born to Randel and Kim Harvey he had a mild case of aspergers and was treated disfairly by other classmates most of his life.

He constantly wanted to prove to the world, and also himself, that he was no pushover and that he can accomplich many achievements, including becoming a great comic book writer.

though his disability was a constant bump in his path he never gave up and contiued to push himself and challenged himself to strive for the top.
