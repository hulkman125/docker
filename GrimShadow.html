Grim Shadow


The grim Shadow was a teenage boy named Gary Oscars who had his family destroyed by the mob who forced him to being sent to the orphanage. During a trip to the science lab he gets infected with chemicals that gave him super human agility. Using his new powers he uses them to fight crime and to avenge the loss of his family.
