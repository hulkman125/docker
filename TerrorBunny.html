Terror Bunny


Terror Bunny was once a normal rabbit that was used for a scientific expirement. The scientists injected in him a special serum to cure certain ailments but something went horribly wrong. The Rabbit became monstarous and started a rampage trying to find its purpose in life. constantly on the hunt by its creators this monster continuasly strives to survive the world.
